Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how RWX-TEK INC. (“OVRTK”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile apps and website (collectively, the “Service”). By using the Service, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1) Information We Collect

Account & Profile: display name, handle, email, and optional bio/links.

Garage Content: cars, parts, notes, photos, costs, and related metadata that you choose to save.

Chat Content: messages you send to Scotty (our AI assistant) and the assistant’s replies.

Device & Usage: app version, device type, basic diagnostics, and log data (e.g., request timestamps, IP address) for security and performance.

Payments: if you purchase a subscription, we receive confirmation from the app store (Apple/Google). We don’t store full payment card details.

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the Service (e.g., sync your garage, show history, enhance chat quality).
  • Operate Scotty: process your prompts and return answers.
  • Maintain safety, security, and integrity, including abuse prevention and troubleshooting.
  • Communicate with you about updates, support, and changes to the Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3) AI & Third-Party Services

To power Scotty, we send your chat prompts (and limited context) to third-party AI model providers to generate responses. Where supported, we configure providers so your data is not used to train their models. We may also use infrastructure or analytics providers (e.g., hosting, crash reports) to operate and improve the Service.

These providers act as processors on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect your information. We only share what’s necessary to provide the Service and follow applicable laws.

4) Data Retention

We retain your account, garage, and chat history so you can access them across sessions. If you delete content or your account, we will delete or anonymize associated data within a reasonable period, subject to legal and operational requirements (e.g., fraud prevention, backups).

5) Your Choices & Rights

  • Access & Update: edit your profile and content within the app.
  • Export: contact us to request a copy of your data.
  • Delete: use the in-app “Delete account” flow or contact us to request deletion.
  • Preferences: you can limit certain data sharing by not using optional features (e.g., links/bio).

Depending on your region, you may have additional rights (e.g., GDPR/CCPA) to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. We will honor applicable requests as required by law.

6) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we work to mitigate risks and monitor for issues.

7) Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13, or the age of consent in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8) International Transfers

We may process and store information in countries outside your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes to our practices or for legal reasons. We will post the updated version here with a new “Last updated” date. If changes are material, we will provide additional notice as appropriate.
