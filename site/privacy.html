Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 12, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to OVRTK. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address and authentication credentials
  • Display name, username/handle, profile photo
  • Bio, location, and social media links (optional)

Garage & Vehicle Data:

  • Vehicle information (make, model, year, VIN)
  • Modifications, parts lists, and maintenance records
  • Photos and videos you upload
  • Cost tracking and value estimates

Usage Data:

  • Chat conversations with Scotty (our AI assistant)
  • App interactions, preferences, and settings
  • Device information and crash reports

Push Notifications

OVRTK may send you push notifications if you grant permission. We use notifications for:

  • Build Reminders: Optional reminders about your vehicle builds
  • Scotty AI Responses: Notifications when our AI assistant responds to your questions
  • Daily Check-ins: Optional daily engagement messages from Scotty (3pm local time)

All notifications are optional and can be controlled individually through the app's Profile → Preferences screen. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app.

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications. Your device push token is stored securely and is only used to send you notifications you've opted into. We do not share your push token with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve the OVRTK service
  • Power Scotty AI chat and vehicle recommendations
  • Send you notifications you've opted into
  • Enable social features (public profiles, sharing)
  • Process subscription payments via RevenueCat
  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase and Google Cloud Platform infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication when available.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, storage, and push notifications
  • OpenAI: Powers Scotty AI chat (chat data may be processed by OpenAI's API)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple Sign In: Optional authentication method

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Firebase Privacy
  • OpenAI Privacy
  • RevenueCat Privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data only in these situations:

  • Public Profiles: If you enable "Public Profile," your handle, bio, and garage may be visible to others
  • Service Providers: With Firebase, OpenAI, and RevenueCat to operate the service
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

Your Rights & Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Correct: Update your profile and garage information anytime
  • Delete: Request account deletion (see Delete Account)
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications, analytics, or public profile features

To exercise these rights, contact us at customertek@rwxtek.com

Children's Privacy

OVRTK is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion for backup and legal purposes. See our Account Deletion page for specific timelines.

International Users

OVRTK is operated from the United States. By using our service, you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in the US, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
